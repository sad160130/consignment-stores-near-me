Our Story: More Than a Directory

Why we started, and what drives us every day.

Our journey began from a simple, personal frustration: we loved the thrill of consignment shopping but found it incredibly difficult to find reliable, up-to-date information about local stores. We knew there were countless hidden gems out there, full of unique treasures and passionate owners.

Driven by a passion for sustainable fashion and supporting small businesses, we decided to build the solution ourselves. In [Year], we launched ConsignmentStores.site not just as a list of stores, but as a community hub—a place for shoppers to discover and for store owners to be seen.

Our mission is to make second-hand the first choice by making it accessible, trustworthy, and exciting for everyone.
